HELP IS ONLY ONE TOUCH AWAY

Smart Home provides 24/7/365, award-winning home security monitoring services. When smoke, fire, or carbon monoxide create life-threatening situations, the one-touch emergency callout button on your control panel puts you in direct contact with monitoring teams, shaving precious seconds off response times.

Cameras

Doorbell Camera detects, auto-records, and notifies you when someone approaches your door. A wide-angle lens and night vision record clear video 24/7, so porch pirates don’t escape undetected. Smart Locks

NEVER WORRY THAT YOU LEFT YOUR DOOR UNLOCKED

Smart Locks let you control your front door on Vivint Smart Home mobile from the gym, the coffee shop, or the office. Plus, you can set mobile alerts for when you forget to lock up. That way hectic mornings don’t become entire days fretting you left the door unlocked.

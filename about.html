Decanto Smarthome is a local family owned business located in Gabriel County Philippines. We have been in the industry for 11 years and relocated to open our new shop in September 2015. We service customers throughout the Philippines. As an authorized dealer for large companies we have become the preferred contact for local customers. We take pride in giving our customers the benefits of our large corporate affiliates. The best in products and service, however we do our own customer service. We feel this gives our customers the best in product and service with the customer service of your friendly neighborhood business.
